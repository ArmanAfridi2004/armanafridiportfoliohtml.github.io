About Me

I am Arman Afridi Rahman, and I completed my Diploma in Computer Technology in 2023. I am currently looking for opportunities in Web Development industry.

In this era of technology every field requires a balanced work environment between humans and machines, I want to be a part of this system where I can work on this equilibrium. When I was in 6th standard we had a new teacher in our school. She taught us a subject called Information and communication technology. She introduced me to this machine where I saw countless potential and many ideas which are yet to be made. I started googling many topics around computer and discovered many fields which it offers. At first, because I used to play games and at that time, YouTube was kind of new. I thought I should make a channel. I made a channel where I uploaded tutorials there I learnt Video editing. I used to merge other audio clips in my video. I watched many tutorials at that time to do it in a way so others can enjoy it. After a while I had to stop making contents because I was interested in another industry. I used to play cricket. It’s a sport in Bangladesh and many other countries. I went to a club called ‘Abahoni’ but soon discovered it didn’t intrigued me as much as computer did. I loved to create things which came out of my mind. So, I started discovering other sectors around it. Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Premiere Pro were the next set of tools which I tried to learn and I still try to use and practice them, so I can make my projects stand out. Then after becoming a student of A.I.T.V.E.T, I was introduced with python and C. Their potential and the way each line of codes can make the things I want to make has encouraged me to learn more. I tried to learn the basics of C, C#, Python, Java. But when I discovered web applications and websites, I started watching tutorials around HTML, CSS. I tried to grasp the basic and core concepts. I learnt there is a need of database and a language which can communicate with the database and bring the data into my html. Then I was introduced with PHP and a framework Laravel. I discovered that in order to make my project more interactive, I had to learn JavaScript. One step led to another, made me focus on one career.
I never knew life will let me learn so many things in such a short time. I tried to hold on to any topic which I understood. Apart from computer, I am intrigued by the fascinating nature of our universe. The human era of this planet evolves very rapidly in comparison with other sentient beings. I think it would be unwise to not update ourselves with the knowledge of our present and form a better future for the next generation. We live in someone else’s world who are yet to come. And I think by learning technology and keeping myself up to date with the knowledge, I can help build a better future for many.
